Better Streets Broward

Advocating for better cities

Our Mission

Better Streets Broward is a grassroots organization dedicated to creating a more affordable, safe, and accessible Broward County. We work to reform zoning for affordable housing, improve street safety, and expand public transit and micromobility options.

About Us

Founded to support the Strong Towns vision, Better Streets Broward is your local voice for smarter growth and sustainable infrastructure. We collaborate with community leaders, advocate for policy changes, and organize events to educate and inspire residents.
